My favorite three dishes at the Sham restaurant

  1. Mixed Grills:

    This consists of a variety of grilled meats over charcoal, such as kebab, shish tawook, and kofta. This dish is very popular for its delicious and distinctive taste.

  2. Sushi:

    Sushi is a Japanese dish made of raw fish, rice, and vinegar sauce, often served on skewers. It's a popular street food that's often found in popular tourist destinations.

  3. Levantine Kibbeh:

    Kibbeh is a traditional and famous dish in Levantine cuisine. It can be eaten either fried or baked, and it is stuffed with minced meat and nuts, often served with yogurt or salads.
