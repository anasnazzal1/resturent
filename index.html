Al-Sham Restaurant

"Al-Sham Restaurant" typically refers to establishments serving Levantine cuisine,
offering traditional dishes from the Levant region, which includes Syria, Lebanon,
Jordan, and Palestine. These restaurants are known for their flavorful and diverse menu,
featuring dishes like grilled meats (kebabs), seafood, manakish, hummus, falafel, and shawarma.
They also offer popular Middle Eastern desserts such as kunafa and baklava.
The atmosphere in Al-Sham restaurants is often warm and inviting,
reflecting the rich cultural heritage and authentic Arabian traditions.
